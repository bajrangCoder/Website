How to download qbasic on android phone?

Hello readers, Today in this page you'll learn how to download qbasic interpreter on android phone. Earlier I have shared a project on How to make Digital clock in html, css, javascript? and now it's time to download qbasic .

STEPS FOR DOWNLOADING: -

  • Download an "dosbox" . On that link ” https://apkgoogle.org/apps/magic-dosbox-v-1-0-79-apk-paid/”
  • Dosbox name: Magic dosbox scroll down and you get a blue button and written on that. button: “Magic DosBox 1.0.79.apk” .
  • Click on button
  • Download started and install it in your phone.
  • Now download a qbasic file . On that link “ https://www.qbasic.net/en/qbasic-downloads/compiler/qbasic-interpreter.htm”
  • Scroll down and you find there are three files you have to download the first one(with a black image).
  • Now extract the downloaded file you find there are two files (QBASIC.EXE, QBASIC.HELP).
  • In your file manager create a folder name: QBASIC
  • And paste two files which we have downloaded previously.
  • Now open DosBox
  • When you open the DosBox you have to all the permission that are required .
  • Then you find three options in the left of the screen . You have to select second option.
  • And choose “New game"
  • Now you set a title: QBASIC
  • And in Hardware section you find a “choose” option click it and choose the Qbasic folder that we have created previously.
  • Scroll down and go to Software section there two choose option you have to click the choose button and select “QBASIC.EXE” in both.
  • Last of the page you find an option click on it.
  • Your Qbasic is now setup
  • Click the QBASIC at the top of the screen and now qbasic is running.
  • Enjoy it!
